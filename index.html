01 How fast will I see results after working with you? +
Most of the work takes 7-14 days to deliver. Once implemented, you'll typically see movement on platform metrics within the first 2-3 weeks, click rate and average order value usually move first, ranking follows within 30-60 days.
02 Do you work with restaurants outside the US and Canada? +
Yes. Uber Eats and DoorDash algorithms work the same way globally. We work with restaurants in any country where these platforms operate. Everything is delivered remotely.
03 What if my restaurant is brand new and not launched yet? +
Tier 3 (Full Package) is built for this. We handle everything from concept to launch, menu, photos, platform setup, positioning. You avoid 6 months of trial and error.
04 Can you guarantee specific revenue numbers? +
No, and anyone who promises specific numbers on delivery platforms is lying. What we guarantee is that every recommendation comes from systems we actually run daily on our own restaurants, not theoretical frameworks.
05 How is this different from hiring a regular marketing agency? +
We're not marketers. We're operators. We've run 4 restaurant concepts across the US and Canada for 6+ years. We know these platforms because we live on them, not because we read about them.
06 What do you need from me to get started? +
Access to your Uber Eats and DoorDash dashboards (read-only is fine), your current menu, and a 30-minute kickoff call. That's it. We handle the rest.
07 How do payments work? +
50% deposit upfront, 50% on delivery. Stripe invoice. That way neither side takes all the risk. The deposit model also makes the service accessible if cash flow is tight.
08 What if the service doesn't work for my restaurant? +
On our first free call, we'll tell you straight if your food quality is the problem, because no optimization fixes that. If you have a good product with weak platform execution, this works. Every time.
